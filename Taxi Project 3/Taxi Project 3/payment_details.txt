All drivers are entitled to a 35% commission  with a bonus commission up to 15% depending on Customer ratings, complaints, and feedback.
In return only 50% - 10% per km of all driving expenses are covered by the company with maintenance costs being completely covered in order to guarantee driver and trip road safety. 
High and consistent customer satisfaction may be subject to receiving a bonus at the end of each financial quarter provided the budget can afford such an expense.
